Statistics

What is it?

Statistics is the study of data collection, analysis, interpretation, presentation, and organization. It helps us understand patterns, trends, and relationships in various fields. Probability, a branch of statistics, deals with uncertainty and likelihood. Key terms include mean, median, mode, standard deviation, and variance

Summarize

What is it?

When you summarize, you restate the most important information from a text using your own words. It’s a powerful reading strategy that increases comprehension and retention of information. Summaries can be written, spoken, dramatic, artistic, visual, physical, or musical. Remember to keep only essential details and avoid personal opinions or judgments. The summary should be 10% to 25% of the original text’s length
